How does Cards Against Humanity works?

Cards Against Humanity is a party game in which players take turns reading out loud a question or statement from a black card. Other players then choose from their hand of white cards to fill in the blank or answer the question. The person who reads the black card then picks the funniest or most outrageous answer from the white cards, and the player who provided that answer wins the round. The game often includes dark or controversial humor and is known for its absurd and sometimes offensive combinations. The goal is to have fun and make everyone laugh.
